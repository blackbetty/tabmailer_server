What is LinkMeLater?

LinkMeLater is a Chrome Extension which helps you manage your open tabs and reading list by:

  1. Saving the tab you're looking at
  2. Closing it
  3. Emailing it to your on a random later date
To get LinkMeLater, download it here! Or, if you already have the extension and an account, check out your dashboard!
